How to Add a New Post (PowerShell)

  node scripts/new-post.cjs --title "Your Post Title" --category market --date 2025-01-15

  Categories: product, announcements, policy, company, social, market

  The script automatically:
  1. Generates slug and ID
  2. Inserts the post at the top of src/data/posts.ts
  3. Creates a placeholder thumbnail SVG
  4. The post appears on /news immediately — no manual index editing

  After running the script, edit src/data/posts.ts to fill in the post content (search for the post ID the script outputs).
